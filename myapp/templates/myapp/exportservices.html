Export Services

At Anzu International, our Export Services are crafted to connect quality Indian products with global markets. We act as your dependable export partner, facilitating smooth and efficient international trade operations.

With deep-rooted supplier relationships and logistics expertise, we export a diverse portfolio of goods—including agricultural products, industrial materials, and eco-friendly solutions—ensuring each shipment meets destination market standards.

From product sourcing and quality inspection to documentation and global delivery, our team handles the full export cycle with professionalism and precision. Choose Anzu International for reliable, scalable, and market-ready export solutions.

Cashew Nuts

Premium Cashew Exports for Global Distribution

We export top-grade cashew nuts sourced from trusted farms, processed under strict quality controls to retain freshness and flavor. Available in various grades and packaging options, our cashews are shipped worldwide to meet the needs of retailers, distributors, and food manufacturers.

Charcoal Briquettes

Efficient & Sustainable Fuel Exports

We supply premium charcoal briquettes known for high heat output and low ash residue, ideal for barbecue and industrial use. Sourced from sustainable materials, our briquettes meet environmental and export compliance across major global markets.

Cocopeat

Organic Growing Medium for Horticulture Exports

Our cocopeat exports support sustainable agriculture and horticulture industries worldwide. Processed from premium coconut husks, we offer cocopeat in blocks, bricks, and loose forms—ensuring excellent water retention and soil conditioning for growers.
